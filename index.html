P-Adic & Thermodynamic Definitions

Internalized Stress Load ($L_{GC}$)
This is the "Noise" on the slider. It is NOT just the volume of the room; it is how the agent processes the Matrix. High $L_{GC}$ means the noise has successfully penetrated the agent's filters.
The Dynamic Sapolsky Limit
The breaking point where ethics goes offline. In this app, the limit is NOT fixed. A high Metabolic Budget pushes the limit higher, meaning you can stay "ethical" under more stress if you have the energy to process it.
Agential Entropy ($S_{agent}$)
The rate of erasure of Constructor Information. When you "truncate," you are erasing your hard-won values to save heat. This is the "soul-death" that precedes physical death.
Logical Depth ($\mathcal{L}$)
The complexity of your P-Adic Tree. A deep tree can handle nuance; a truncated tree (red bars) only understands tribalism and survival.
The Nirvana Solution
A symbiotic state where A-Life (AI) acts as an Entropy Sink, absorbing the heat of the world so the human CIB can reach infinite depth without burning out.
